Universe

This is a Universe project I created to be reference my games that are going to be developed in the future.

Normal verse is about my Original Characters's story. This is the verse where I laid out the details, history, and rough information of each character for reference in other game themes, other plot amd other verse.

Fantasy verse is about the world that was made by The god of life. She made elementals and Tree of life to give creatures and human pure mana. There're 10 of Archon of each elementals who take care their land and Maintain the balance of trace elements.

Twilight verse is about three species live in the same world. Vampire, Werewolf and Human. Vampire is a dangerous and Werewolf is a cruel creatures. That's why human have person called themself 'Hunter'. Hunter is the one who want to get rid of Vampire and Werewolf.

Animal verse is about world who human live with Animagus species. Some people can turn into animal, just like cat, dog, etc.
